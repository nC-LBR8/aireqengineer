# US-002: [User Story] Grid-based snake movement and growth
Status: todo
Priority: medium
Requirement-Refs: REQ-001
Tags: Functional

## Description
As a player, I want the snake to move predictably on a grid, respond to direction input, and grow when eating food.

## Acceptance Criteria
- Snake occupies discrete grid cells; on each logic tick the head moves to the next cell in current direction.
- Direction changes from the player apply no later than the next tick and disallow instant 180° reversals.
- When food is consumed, snake length increases by one cell on the next tick and score updates.
