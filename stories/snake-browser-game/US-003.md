# US-003: [User Story] Food spawning and score tracking
Status: todo
Priority: medium
Requirement-Refs: REQ-001
Tags: Functional

## Description
As a player, I want food to appear on free cells and my score to increase when food is eaten.

## Acceptance Criteria
- New food spawns on a random grid cell that is not occupied by the snake.
- Eating food increments score by a defined amount (default +10) and spawns another food within one tick.
- Current score is visible in the UI and resets on restart.
