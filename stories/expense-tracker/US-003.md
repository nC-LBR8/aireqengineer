# US-003: [User Story] Filter expenses by month and category
Status: todo
Priority: high
Requirement-Refs: REQ-001
Feature-Refs: FEAT-002
Tags: Functional

## Description
As a user, I want to filter expenses by month and category so that I can review a focused subset of my spending.

## Acceptance Criteria
- The app provides a month filter that limits the visible expense list to records from the selected month.
- The app provides a category filter that limits the visible expense list to records in the selected category.
- The month and category filters can be applied together.
- Changing or clearing a filter updates the visible list immediately.
