# US-004: [User Story] View monthly totals and category breakdown
Status: todo
Priority: high
Requirement-Refs: REQ-001
Feature-Refs: FEAT-002
Tags: Functional

## Description
As a user, I want to see the monthly total and a simple category breakdown so that I can understand where my money goes.

## Acceptance Criteria
- The app shows the total amount for the currently selected month.
- The app shows a category breakdown for the currently selected month using the expenses in scope.
- The monthly total and category breakdown update when expenses are added, edited, deleted, or filtered.
- The sum of the category breakdown values matches the monthly total for the currently selected month.
